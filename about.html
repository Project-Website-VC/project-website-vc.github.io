About

Background, research focus, and community work.

Bio

I work at the intersection of information, data, and society, focusing on trustworthy AI, conversational systems, and human-computer interaction. My goal is to bridge sociotechnical divides for community well-being and fair decision-making.

My recent efforts include community outreach for library and information professionals on the ethical application of AI and moderating discussions on social media research and practice.

Research Interests

  • Bidirectional nature of bias in LLMs and methods for mitigation
  • Conversational IR and explainable, dialog-centric retrieval
  • Human-AI interaction and evaluation methods
  • Ethical AI
  • Conversational Agents
  • Human-AI Interaction

Teaching & Mentorship

  • AI Basics (AI 101): Fundamentals for working professionals; minimal programming required.
  • Information Retrieval System Design: Ethics-forward, tool-based approach.
